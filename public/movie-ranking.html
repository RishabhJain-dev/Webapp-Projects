The Best 2023 Bollywood Movies According to Rishabh

Top 3 Movies

Sirf Ek Banda Kafi Hai

The best social message movie. Great Story telling act by Manoj Bajpayee.

Mission Majnu

Amazing Spy movie by Siddharth Malhotra. The story keeps you entwined till the end.

Sam Bahadur

Biopic of India's legendary Field Marshal Sam Manekshaw. Great work of art by Vicky Kaushal, dived too deep into the character. Felt like sir I watching Sir Sam Manekshaw live.
